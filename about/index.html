About TripCalc

TripCalc is part of the WiseClick family of free online tools. We provide travel planning calculators to help you make smarter decisions about your trips.

Our Tools

We offer 10 free travel tools covering budget planning, currency conversion, fly vs drive comparisons, expense splitting, jet lag estimation, travel insurance, packing lists, timezone conversion, rewards optimization, and driving cost calculation.

Our Mission

We believe travel planning should be simple and accessible. Our tools run entirely in your browser — no sign-up, no data collection, no hidden fees. Just accurate calculations you can trust.

Disclaimer

TripCalc tools are for estimation purposes only. Results are not financial or travel advice. Please verify all costs and requirements with official sources before making travel decisions.
